Annavaram

Only Red Wheat Semolina should be used. For a cup of Wheat Semolina, one cup Sugar, one cup Grated Jaggery, 3-4 cups of water. You can use Brown Sugar, if you like.
Usually they use both Sugar and Jaggery for the Prasadam but you could make it with just Jaggery too.
