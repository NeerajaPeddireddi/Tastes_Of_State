Ramachandrapuram

Artos is a regional soft drink and the first aerated soft drink in the state of Andhra Pradesh.
It was first named as "Ramachandra Raju soft drinks" and later renamed as "A.R Raju Tonics" and later converted into Artos.
It was originated in Ramachandrapuram in East Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh
