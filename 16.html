Kakinada

Kakinada Kaja is a sweet delicacy originated from the city of Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh, India in 1891.
It is different from the regular world known Khaja. It resembles a closed Tube.
Tube is known as Gottam in Telugu. So it was called as Gottam Kaja. It has its own recipe.
