Marturu

The biryani stuffed inside the thin, golden-yellow omelette is also not your regular biryani, it is brimming with the goodness of juicy mutton keema and spicy prawns. The omelette is made in a heavy kadhai and seasoned with red chilli powder.
Once cooked, it is carefully scraped out and placed on a plate, the biryani is placed right in the centre and the edges are folded and the entire omelette is used to nicely wrap the biryani.
This ‘wrap’ is then served hot with a spicy curry or salan and raita.
